Volcano Snails
Scientific name: Chrysomallon Squamiferum
Common names: Scaly-Foot Gastropod, Volcano Snail
Habitat/Range: Deep-sea vents in the Indian Ocean

Look at this snail, isn't it so metal?!?!?! Literally, it's covered in iron. These snails are exclusively found around hydrothermal vents in the Indian Ocean. They absorb metals spewed by these vents and incorporate them into their bodies, leading to the scales on their feet and the iron covering on their shells. They also have very large hearts for their size, taking up about 4% of their body volume, due to the low oxygen in their environment.
